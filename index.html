Aim of the game:
A team must guess the five 'answer words' in the Word List panel based on 'clue words' given by the team's clue giver in less than 45 seconds to win.
Team setup:
Each team comprises of three or more people. For each round one of the team members will be the clue giver. The host of the game will hold the play device and will be the judge, jury and scorekeeper.
Game procedure:
1. One member from each team will be the clue giver and join the host.
2. The host taps the New Card button on the New Card panel. This will reset the game for this round.
3. The two clue givers will bid for the lowest number of 'clue words' they will use to get their team to guess the five 'answer words' within 45 seconds. The host will use the slider in the Word's Left panel to change the number of words being bid (25 to 10 words). The one with the lowest bid of 'clue words' starts the guessing part of the game.
4. The guessing part of the game starts by the host tapping on the Start Countdown button on the Countdown Timer panel. Everytime the clue giver gives a clue, tap the Count Words button on the Word's Left panel. If the team guess the correct 'answer word' tap the red numbered button next to the 'answer word' and it will change to green and sound a 'ding'
5. If all five 'answer words' have been guessed before the timer counts down to zero or the word's left reaches less than zero, the 'WON' flag will appear in the New Card panel and sound a 'Hurray! dingy'.
6. If either the timer counts down to zero or the word's left is less than zero, the 'LOSE' flag will appear in the New Card panel and sound a 'buzzer'.
Scoring:
250 points for the team that wins the round.
